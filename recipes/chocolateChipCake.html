How to make a chocolate chip cake?

Description

Super moist and decadent, but easy-to-make cake! Dust with confectioner's sugar if desired.

Preparation details

Timing

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hr
  • Total Time:
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 1 (10-inch) Bundt cake

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 516
  • Fat: 35g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Protein: 6g

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix
  • 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease ans flour a Bundt pan.
  2. Combine cake mix, pudding mix, oil, eggs, water, sour cream, adn vanilla. Beat until smooth. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Allow to cool.
